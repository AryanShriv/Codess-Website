Objectives

Rigorous Coding

A carefully consolidated list of 350+ coding questions covering and strengthening the basics of interview programming is provided. The questions help form a strong base for FAANG and other MNCs.

A carefully consolidated list of coding questions strengthening the basics of programming is provided.

Mock Interviews

Weekly mocks are conducted to provide an atmosphere similar to an actual interview which helps increase confidence and comfort. Participants with frequent mocks grab jobs quicker on average.

Mocks replicate actual interviews very closely which helps increase confidence and comfort.

Weekly Catch-ups

With weekly online group meetings participants share their developments and shortcomings and probable progress strategies are discussed. Weekly catch-ups give ample time to test if prep strategy works and possible amends.

Weekly catch-ups give ample time to test if prep strategy works and possible amends.

Progress Tracking

Weekly meetings are used to gauge progress over the week. The progress is compared with previous weeks to anaylze improvement and growth curve and decide on changed requires to meet targets.

Weekly meetings are used to gauge progress over the week and decide on changed requires to meet targets.

Group Support

The presence of a group of individuals with similar goals yet different backgrounds and skill sets helps in exchange of ideas and varied ways to hone personal skills.

Groups help in exchangeof ideas and varied ways to hone personal skills.

Quick Consulting

Availability of a quick and experienced mentor is required when competing in the job market. Personal 24/7 help is provided to all the participants without fail.

Personal 24/7 help is provided to all the participants without fail.
